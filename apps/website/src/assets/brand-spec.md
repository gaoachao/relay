# Relay — Website Brand Notes

## Name

- Brand: **Relay**
- Descriptor: **Interface Translator**
- English line: **A better interface for what’s in front of you.**
- Chinese line: **为眼前的设备，生成更好用的界面。**

Relay describes the product’s job: relaying a dense physical interface into one clear, task-focused action for the person in front of it.

## Mark

- Source: `relay-app-icon.svg`
- Meaning: three physical controls relay into one clear, accessible action
- Master canvas: 1024 × 1024, full bleed
- Platform masks provide final icon corners; never bake rounded corners into the source

## Color

- Brand mark blue: `#007AFF`
- Accessible UI blue on light surfaces: `#0066CC`
- Mark on primary: `#FFFFFF`
- Ink: `#1D1D1F`
- Secondary text: `#6E6E73`
- Soft surface: `#F5F5F7`

Do not add gradients, glows, extra accent hues, or internal shadows.

## Website lockup

Use the SVG mark next to the live-text wordmark `Relay`. Keep “Interface Translator” as a descriptor, not part of the icon.

## Avoid

- Do not use previous project names.
- Do not stretch, rotate, outline, or recolor individual paths.
- Do not place text inside the app icon.
- Do not use the mark as decorative wallpaper.
